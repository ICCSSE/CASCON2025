Off-Campus Downtown Toronto

In Downtown Toronto, there are many hotels you can choose from. We do not have any specific recommendation or negotiated rate at this time. If you choose to stay Downtown, accommodations on or close to University Av., between Bloor and Front St. are the closest to Subway Line 1, which takes you directly to York University. The subway is safe, convenient, and used daily by York students, professors, and staff.
