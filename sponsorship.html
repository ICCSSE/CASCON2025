Sponsorship and Opportunities

About CASCON

The IEEE International Conference on Collaborative Advances in Software and COmputiNg (CASCON) is a premier academic and industrial conference for advanced studies in computer science and software engineering. Since 1991, CASCON has attracted software developers, researchers, innovators, technologists and decision-makers from academia, industry, and government, who come together to learn about technology trends, present papers, participate in workshops, and exhibit prototypes and solutions. The conference program features thought-provoking keynotes, original research papers, stimulating workshops, and an engaging technology expo. Conference proceedings are published in the IEEE XPlore. We are happy to announce that this year CASCON will be co-sponsor by IEEE Computer Society and CS-CAN/INFO-CAN,, aiming to promote knowledge sharing, networking, and innovation in Canadian computer science and related fields.

Corporate Support Opportunities

The 35th IEEE CASCON conference – which will be held at York University, Toronto, Canada, November 10-13, 2025 – welcomes corporate support. With over 250 expected participants from academia and industry, CASCON 2025 is an ideal venue for raising awareness of relevant products and services, networking with potential research collaborators, academics, students and potential hires, while being exposed to new ideas that push the state of the art of emerging software systems and architectures, evolving software engineering practices, innovations in AI, cybersecurity and digital privacy, human-computer interaction (HCI) & immersive experiences, and sustainable and responsible computing. If you are interested in benefiting from all that CASCON 2025 has to offer through sponsorship or you have any further questions please do not hesitate to contact the General Co-Chairs, Hausi Müller (University of Victoria) and Ying Zou (Queen’s University) or the Sponsorship Co-Chairs, Sedef Kocac (Vector Institute) and Kelly Lyons (University of Toronto). We are happy to discuss partnership opportunities.
